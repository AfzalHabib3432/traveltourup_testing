### <span style="color:#16a34a">Section 1: Executive Summary</span>

**Our Competitive Edge:**
1. **Niche down hard** — dominate 1–2 categories, not generalist
2. **Price with fees built in** — 10–20% platform fees absorbed into margins
3. **Invest in profile building first** — algorithms favor established profiles
4. **Compete on value & speed** — never on being cheapest
5. **Multiple profiles across platforms** — strict compliance to avoid suspension

**Key Success Factors:** Expert-guided strategy (3–4 consultations) · Optimized profiles on 2–3 platforms · Specialist bidders with proven records · Clear SOPs & daily accountability · Pricing that profits after fees · Rapid first-project delivery for reviews

**Expected Timeline to Revenue:**

| Best Case | Week 1–2 | Week 2–3 | Week 3–4 |
| Realistic | Week 3–4 | Week 4–6 | Week 6–8 |
| Worst Case | Week 6–8 | Week 8–12 | Week 10–14 |

**Our Target:** First project Week 2–3 after active bidding (Week 6–7 from today).

### <span style="color:#16a34a">Section 7: SOPs & Workflow</span>

**Daily Bidding Workflow (Per Bidder — US Hours):**
1. **6:00 PM PKT** — Check-in: messages, notifications, yesterday's review
2. **6:30 PM** — Job scanning (45 min): shortlist 25–35 relevant jobs
3. **7:15 PM** — Proposal writing (3–4 hrs): submit 15–25 customized proposals
4. **11:15 PM** — Follow-up (30 min): 2–3 day old proposals, update CRM
5. **12:00 AM** — End-of-day report to Team Lead

**Proposal Structure (6 Parts):** Personalized Hook (1–2 lines) → Understand Their Problem (2–3 lines) → Your Solution (3–5 bullets) → Proof + Portfolio Link → Pricing & Timeline (specific) → Call to Action

**Proposal Quality Standards:** Must reference specific job post detail · 150–250 words (300–400 for high-value) · Zero grammar errors · 1–2 relevant portfolio examples · Clear pricing/range · Professional, confident tone

**Job Selection Priorities:**
- **A (Must Bid):** $1,000+ budget, client hiring history, exact service, <15 proposals
- **B (Should Bid):** $500–$999, verified client, related service, <25 proposals
- **C (Consider):** $300–$499, new client, general match
- **Skip:** Under $300, poor reviews, vague scope, outside capabilities

**Escalation Matrix:** Scope change → PM (immediately) · Negative review threat → Leadership (1 hr) · Technical question → Tech Lead (2 hrs) · Discount >20% → Team Lead (before responding) · Suspension notice → Leadership (immediately)

**Won Bid → Delivery Handoff:** Bidder wins → Team Lead reviews → PM assigned → Kickoff call → Project created in ClickUp/Asana → Delivery team executes → PM manages communication → QA → Client review → Final delivery → Request review → Finance collects payment

**Bidder Targets:**

| Metric | Daily | Weekly | Monthly |
|--------|-------|--------|---------|
| Proposals submitted | 15–25 | 75–125 | 300–500 |
| Client responses | 2–5 | 10–25 | 40–100 |
| Projects won | — | 1–2 | 4–8 |

**Pricing Psychology:** Never be cheapest · Use specific numbers ($2,750 > $3,000) · Offer 2–3 tiers · Anchor high ("US agencies charge $8K; we deliver at $3,500") · Include timeline in price · Show ROI

### <span style="color:#16a34a">Section 8: Pricing Strategy</span>

**Formula:** `Minimum Bid = (Delivery Cost + Overhead) ÷ (1 - Platform Fee %) ÷ (1 - Target Profit %)`

| Service | Floor | Standard | Premium | Min. Margin |
|---------|-------|----------|---------|-------------|
| Simple Website (5 pg) | $1,200 | $2,000–$3,500 | $4,000–$6,000 | 35%+ |
| WordPress Custom | $2,000 | $3,500–$6,000 | $7,000–$12,000 | 40%+ |
| SEO Audit | $300 | $500–$1,000 | $1,500–$2,500 | 40%+ |
| Monthly SEO | $500/mo | $800–$1,500/mo | $2,000–$3,000/mo | 45%+ |
| Logo Design | $150 | $300–$600 | $800–$1,500 | 50%+ |
| Landing Page | $500 | $800–$1,500 | $2,000–$3,000 | 40%+ |
| E-commerce (Shopify) | $2,000 | $3,500–$6,000 | $8,000–$15,000 | 35%+ |
| Mobile App (Basic) | $5,000 | $8,000–$15,000 | $20,000–$40,000 | 35%+ |

**Pricing by Profile Maturity:** New (0–5 reviews): Floor, 20–25% margin · Growing (5–15): Standard, 30–40% · Established (15+): Premium, 40–50% · Top Rated: Premium+, 45–55%

**Red Lines (Always Decline):** Below floor price · 50%+ discount requests · Vague scope, won't clarify · Off-platform payment · Technology we lack

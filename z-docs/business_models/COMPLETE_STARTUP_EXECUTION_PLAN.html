Section 12: Proposal Writing Framework

Purpose: Standardized 6-part structure for winning proposals
#PartWhat to Write
1Personalized Hook1–2 sentences referencing something specific from the job post
2Understand Problem2–3 sentences restating their need — show you understand the pain
3Your Solution3–5 specific bullet points of what you'll deliver
4Proof / Social Proof1–2 sentences + link to relevant portfolio or results
5Pricing & TimelineExact price, exact days — optional 2–3 tier approach
6Call to ActionSpecific next step — call, message, or calendly link
Pricing Psychology Tips:
  1. 1.Never be the cheapest — cheapest = lowest quality perception
  2. 2.Use specific numbers — "$2,750" beats "$3,000"
  3. 3.Offer 2–3 tiers (Basic/Standard/Premium)
  4. 4.Anchor high: "US agencies charge $8K; we deliver same at $3,500"
  5. 5.Show ROI: "$1,500 SEO → $5K–$10K new business in 6 months"

Section 17: Commonly Missed Items — Critical Checklist

Purpose: Avoid the 22 mistakes most teams make when launching bidding operations
Pre-Launch (6 Items):
  1. 1.Profile verification takes 3–7 days (Fiverr Pro: weeks) — start Day 1
  2. 2.Connect economics not tracked — budget burns fast without monitoring
  3. 3.No portfolio before bidding — build 6+ pieces during planning month
  4. 4.Timezone mismatch — bidders must work US hours (6 PM – 2 AM PKT)
  5. 5.No NDA/contract with bidders — sign before giving any access
  6. 6.No backup for suspension — maintain 2–3 active profiles
Operational (6 Items):
  1. 7.Delivery team not ready when projects won — pre-assign capacity
  2. 8.No scope document template — vague scope = scope creep = loss
  3. 9.Ignoring JSS — below 90% kills visibility, monitor weekly
  4. 10.Not requesting reviews — clients don't leave them unless asked
  5. 11.No upsell strategy — create post-delivery upsell workflow
  6. 12.Not tracking which proposal styles win — tag and analyze
Financial (5 Items):
  1. 13.Platform fees not factored into pricing
  2. 14.Revision costs not budgeted — "unlimited revisions" = unlimited cost
  3. 15.Currency fluctuation — price in USD, pay in PKR, monitor monthly
  4. 16.Tax obligations — consult advisor for Pakistan + US implications
  5. 17.Payment delays (Upwork: 10 days, Fiverr: 7–14) — maintain buffer
Strategic (5 Items):
  1. 18.No exit from platform dependency — transition clients by Month 12
  2. 19.Not building email list — collect emails for direct outreach
  3. 20.Ignoring Clutch/website — invest from Month 1 for passive leads
  4. 21.No competitive intelligence — monthly competitor review
  5. 22.Bidder burnout — rotate categories, provide templates, manage workload
